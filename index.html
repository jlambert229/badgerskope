Evidence You Can Actually Trust

Every claim in BadgerSkope is tagged with its evidence tier so you never confuse marketing with proof.

Approved / Labeled

FDA or regulator-approved. Backed by official prescribing information.

Major Trials

Large, high-quality clinical trials in humans.

Early Human

Small human studies or Phase 1 safety data.

Animal Only

Tested in animals; human translation uncertain.

Practice-Based

Clinic patterns or regional prescribing; controlled data is thin.

We don't hide weak evidence — we label it clearly so you can decide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BadgerSkope free?

Yes, completely. The library is open and always will be.

Is this medical advice?

No. BadgerSkope is a research literacy tool. It summarizes published science in plain English but is not a substitute for professional medical guidance.

Where does the data come from?

From published clinical trials, FDA labels, PubMed, and peer-reviewed research. Each entry links to its sources. Marketing claims are clearly labeled as such.

Can I trust the evidence grades?

The grades tell you the type and quality of evidence behind each claim. We don't inflate — if something only has mouse data, we say so.

Do you sell peptides?

No. BadgerSkope is purely informational. We have no commercial relationship with any peptide vendor.

How do I report an error?

Open an issue on our GitHub repository. We take accuracy seriously.
