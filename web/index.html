HOW TO USE THE LIBRARY.

BadgerSkope is education, not advice. Here's how to navigate the files.

BROWSING

Search
Type any word to search across titles, summaries, benefits, and dosing notes.
Refine
Click REFINE for filters by wellness area, substance type, evidence tier, sort order, and grouping.
Tier chips
Each card shows a tier letter (A–F). A = FDA-approved or strong human trials. F = unconfirmed.
Reset
The CLEAR button next to search appears whenever a filter is active.

READING AN ENTRY

Open detail
Click any card to read the full audit — evidence in context, source quality, dosing notes, side-effect profile, and synergy data.
Sport flags
WADA-banned compounds show an amber flag on the card and a banner in the detail view.
Glossary tooltips
Hover over units (mg, mcg, IU) and acronyms (HbA1c, IGF-1, BDNF) for plain-English definitions.

SELECTING & COMPARING

Select
Use the checkbox on each card or hit SELECT ALL in the toolbar.
Compare
With 2+ entries selected, switch to the COMPARE tab. Differences are highlighted; "best" badges mark strongest evidence.
Stack synergy
The compare view also shows which selected compounds are discussed together in research or product lines.

PERSONAL TOOLS

Bookmarks
Star any card to bookmark it; press b in the detail view. Toggle bookmarked-only with f.
Notes
Each detail view has a private-notes section, saved in your browser only.
Recently viewed
Your last 6 viewed entries surface above the grid for quick re-access.
Share & print
Copy a direct link or print a clean version from the detail toolbar.
